A bit about me

I’m Kosisochukwu Igbokwe, a final-year Computer Science student at the University of Calgary (GPA 3.9 / 4.0), born in Nigeria and based in Canada. I research human-computer interaction and data visualization at the Interactions Lab, and I’ve shipped production code as a full-stack intern.

I care deeply about thoughtful UX and clean engineering — the kind of work where design and code reinforce each other. When I’m not in the lab, I’m building side projects, learning new tools, and breaking things (productively).
